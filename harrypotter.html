Harry Potter Moments

1. Harry’s discovery of his magical heritage

Harry discovers his true nature as a wizard and is introduced to the magical world by Hagrid, the Hogwarts gamekeeper.

2. The entrance to Hogwarts

Harry boards the Hogwarts Express from Platform 9¾ at King’s Cross Station, marking the beginning of his journey into the world of magic.

3. The sorting ceremony

Harry, along with other new students, is sorted into Hogwarts houses, eventually ending up in Gryffindor.

4. The cloak of invisibility

Harry receives his father’s cloak of invisibility for Christmas

5. The first flight lesson

Harry demonstrates his natural talent in flying on broom during his first flight lesson with Madame Hooch.

6. The Mirror of Lusts

Wandering around the school at night, Harry comes across a strange mirror, inside which he sees his dead parents for the first time

7. The Three-Headed Dog

To access the Philosopher’s Stone, Harry, Ron, and Hermione must overcome a series of obstacles. The first is a three-headed dog that acts as a guardian to the trap door that leads to the room where it is hidden

8. The game of magic chess

Another of the obstacles is a game of magic chess

9. The revelation about Quirrell and Voldemort

Harry discovers that the real enemy is Voldemort and that he hides behind Professor Quirrell and decides to sacrifice himself to protect the Philosopher’s Stone and his friends.

10. The triumph of Harry

Harry manages to defeat Quirrell/Voldemort thanks to the protection of his mother and the strength of love, saving the Philosopher’s Stone and ensuring the safety of Hogwarts.
